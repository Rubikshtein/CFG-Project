Who I am

I'm Änki, an expat who moved to Scotland with a passion for cooking and the hobby of cold water surfing. Here, I combine my love for the sea and culinary exploration. Join me on my journey as I share gluten-free and vegan recipes from around the world, including curries, tagines, bowls, porridge, pizza, pasta, and a variety of cakes and muffins.
